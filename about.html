How to Use Our App

The Book-List-Organizer app is your ultimate tool for managing your reading lists effortlessly. Leveraging the Google Books API, it offers a wide range of book options across various genres. With a user-friendly interface, it helps you discover new books, organize your reading lists, and track your reading progress with ease.

About

Our app provides a seamless experience for book enthusiasts, making it easy to find, track, and organize your reading journey. Here’s how it works:

Home Page Overview

The Home Page is your central hub for discovering books. It displays recommended, top-rated, and newly released books across genres like Thriller, Romance, Mystery, and Fantasy.

Search Functionality

Use the search bar to look for books by title or author. View detailed information, including cover image, author, publisher, and description. Add books to your personalized lists with a single tap.

Reading Lists Management

Easily manage your books by organizing them into categories such as Favourite, Currently Reading, and Read. Move books between categories and delete them when necessary, with confirmation prompts to ensure intentional actions.

FAQ

Got questions? We have answers. Here are some frequently asked questions to help you get started:

Q: How do I search for a book?

A: Use the search bar at the top of the Home Page to search for books by title or author. Tap on a book to view its details.

Q: How can I add a book to my reading list?

A: On the book details page, tap the “Add to List” button and select the desired category.

Q: Can I move a book between lists?

A: Yes, simply tap the book in your list, select “Move to”, and choose the new category.

Q: How do I delete a book from my list?

A: Tap the book you want to delete, select “Remove”, and confirm your action.

Q: What information is available on the book details page?

A: You’ll find the cover image, author, publisher, description, and options to add the book to your lists.

Q: How do I get book recommendations?

A: The Home Page automatically displays recommended, top-rated, and newly released books based on your preferences.
